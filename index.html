Our comprehensive selection of quality furniture ensures a wide range of prices – always competitive, whether it’s a statement piece or practical, affordable style.  Fabric choices range from eye catching and stunning to understated elegance, with an extensive range of wear options.  Wood finishes are available in teak, mahogany, cherry, yew and solid oak.  Our service is second to none.

Compact Ranges

Specialist compact ranges for the smaller home or apartment – ideal for those downsizing or creating additional living space (a garden room, conservatory, office or even a ‘granny flat’) within the home.

Recliners

A large selection of recliners on display (small, medium and large sizes); manual actions, power recliners and lift/tilt models.  All available with fixed and reclining settees to match – 2 and 3 seaters – everything in fabric or leather.

Handmade Coil-sprung Chairs and Sofas

Finest quality handmade coil-sprung chairs and sofas, which can be adapted to your size requirements.  3-seaters, 2½-seaters and 2-seaters.  Most with ladies' and gents' chair options.   Fabric and hide groups, sofa beds, three piece suites, swivel recliners, cottage style and show-wood frame suites.

Dining Furniture

Wall units, dressers, display cabinets and sideboards. Available in teak, mahogany, yew tree, cherry and solid oak finishes.  All with an extensive range of dining tables, dining chairs and occasional pieces to match.
